## Workshop on Asymptotic Stability of Solitons — Punta Arenas, Chile

This workshop brought together researchers working on asymptotic stability of solitons and nonlinear dispersive equations. It was held in Punta Arenas, Chile, at the southern tip of Patagonia.

**5–9 January 2026**
